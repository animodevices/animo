01Manifesto

Dogs don't complain. Animo notices changes before symptoms — and tells you.

Every dog is unique. Animo learns your dog's personal rhythm — and then compares every day against his own baseline, not a breed average.

02What it notices

signals.

01

Sleep

Depth, interruptions, night-time restlessness.

02

Breathing

Resting respiratory rate — one of the earliest markers of change.

03

Activity

Movement, play and rest — compared with his usual day.

04

Personal baseline

In the first weeks Animo learns his rhythm — and spots deviations from it, not from an average.

Night is the body's most honest hour

Animo builds a model of the body and turns data into plain language: what's happening and what to do.

First signs of illness

Usually

Barney meets you at the door every day with his favourite toy.

Animo noticed

Over two days activity dropped by 28% and rest grew by almost two hours.

What the owner did

Booked a vet visit. The check-up found early inflammation — caught at an early stage.

02

Stress after moving

The situation

After the move it seems the dog has already settled in.

Animo shows

He barely sleeps at night and moves noticeably less during the day.

What it gives you

Understanding that he's stressed — and helping him adapt sooner.
